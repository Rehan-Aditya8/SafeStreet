How the System Works

A seamless 4-step process from issue detection to final resolution

1
📱

1. Report

Citizens capture road damage using their phone's camera and submit GPS-tagged reports.

2
🤖

2. AI Detection

Our AI instantly categorizes the severity of the damage and classifies it by type.

3
✔️

3. Verification

Govt. officials verify reports and prioritize them based on severity & location.

4
🔨

4. Repair

Contractors receive work orders and repairs are tracked to completion.

System Features

🧠

AI Detection

Advanced neural networks identify pothole patterns, crack formations, and surface deterioration with high accuracy.

📡

Real-Time Tracking

Live dashboards for citizens and officials to track report statuses and repair operations in real-time.

📍

GPS Reporting

Precise GPS tagging while you report road damage ensures faster response from nearby maintenance crews.

Contractor Assignment

Intelligent dispatch of verified contractors to nearby job sites, with schedules and deadlines.
